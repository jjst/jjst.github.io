Building a photobooth in Python

Mon 01 August 2016

Me and Philip (@philj0st) marked the occasion by building a photobooth. Guests could use it to snap pics which then got posted to our internal chat system. This is the story of how we managed to build it all from the ground up the afternoon before the party.
